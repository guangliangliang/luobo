# 怪物四帧合图提示词

用于 AI 生成怪物走路动画资源。每个怪物生成一张横向四帧合图，后续导入 Godot 后按 4 等分裁切成走路动画。

## 通用生成设置

- 推荐比例：16:9
- 推荐用途：图片生成，不用视频生成
- 推荐风格：卡通、插画、游戏素材
- 推荐背景：透明背景 PNG
- 画面结构：一行四列，四个等宽格子
- 帧与帧之间至少留 60 像素透明空白
- 每个动作必须完全待在自己的格子里，不要跨到相邻格子
- 腿部动作要形成流畅连续的走路循环，不要四帧都像同一个站姿
- 第 1 帧和第 3 帧要明显左右脚交替，第 2 帧和第 4 帧作为过渡帧
- 每张图只生成一个怪物的四个走路动作
- 四帧角色必须保持同一个脸、服装、颜色、体型和位置

如果 AI 不稳定，可以优先保留“exactly one row and exactly four equal columns”、“Leave at least 60 pixels of empty transparent padding between each frame”和“The leg poses must form a smooth continuous walking cycle”这几句话。

## 小偷 `thief_sheet.png`

```text
Create ONE transparent PNG image containing a horizontal 4-frame walking sprite sheet for a sneaky village thief enemy. The image must have exactly one row and exactly four equal columns. Each column contains one full-body pose of the same thief character. Leave at least 60 pixels of empty transparent padding between each frame. Each thief pose must stay fully inside its own column and must not overlap into neighboring columns. The leg poses must form a smooth continuous walking cycle, not four similar standing poses. Frame 1: left foot forward with right foot back. Frame 2: body centered passing pose with feet close under the body. Frame 3: right foot forward with left foot back. Frame 4: body centered passing pose leading smoothly back to frame 1. The thief is a thin adult male wearing dark simple Chinese rural cloth clothes, small headscarf, carrying a stolen cloth bundle over one shoulder, sly expression, quick cautious walking motion. Keep the same face, outfit, colors, body size, and position in all four frames. 3/4 view facing right, centered full body in every frame, consistent scale, transparent background, no background, no ground, no shadow, no text, no extra characters. 2D cartoon tower defense game asset, Chinese rural village defense style, clean readable silhouette, not realistic, not bloody.
```
