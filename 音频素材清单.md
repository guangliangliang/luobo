## 🎼 背景音乐 (BGM)

### 1. 主菜单/关卡选择界面音乐
**使用场景：** 玩家刚打开游戏，在主菜单浏览关卡时
**建议时长：** 2-3分钟（循环）
**风格：** 宁静、乡村、中世纪风格

```
[Verse]
Peaceful medieval village background music, acoustic guitar and gentle flute, calm and relaxing atmosphere, subtle countryside ambient sounds in background, no heavy drums, warm and inviting, 80-90 BPM, acoustic folk style, loopable

[Chorus]
Nostalgic village theme, comforting and homely, perfect for a tower defense game's main menu, makes the player feel welcome and ready to defend their village
```

---

### 2. 战斗场景音乐
**使用场景：** 玩家进入关卡，开始防御战时
**建议时长：** 3-4分钟（循环）
**风格：** 紧张但不过于激烈，有节奏感

```
[Verse]
Epic but not overwhelming tower defense battle music, rhythmic hand percussion and lute, subtle orchestral elements, building tension throughout, medieval fantasy style, moderate pace, 120-130 BPM, loopable, not too distracting so player can focus

[Chorus]
Determined and heroic battle theme that motivates the player, balance between action and strategy, feel of defending your home against invaders
```

---

### 3. 关卡胜利音乐
**使用场景：** 成功抵御所有波次，关卡胜利时
**建议时长：** 30-45秒
**风格：** 欢快、庆祝、胜利感

```
Triumphant victory fanfare, celebratory medieval style music with brass and strings, happy and satisfying conclusion, short but memorable, no lyrics, 30-40 seconds, victorious and rewarding feel
```

---

### 4. 关卡失败音乐
**使用场景：** 村庄被怪物攻陷，关卡失败时
**建议时长：** 30-45秒
**风格：** 略带悲伤但不沮丧，鼓励再试一次

```
Gentle defeat theme, somber but not depressing, medieval woodwinds and harp, accepting atmosphere that encourages the player to try again, hopeful undertones, no lyrics, 30-40 seconds, not overly negative
```

---

## 🔊 音效 (SFX)

### ⚠️ 重要提示
**Suno主要擅长音乐，对于音效（SFX）建议用以下工具：**
- **AudioGen** - Meta官方音效生成
- **Stable Audio** - 高质量音效生成
- **Freesound.org** - 直接下载现成的免费游戏音效

---

### 1. 建造防御塔
**使用场景：** 玩家点击空地，选择塔类型后建造
**触发位置：** `build_manager.gd` 中的 `build_tower()` 函数
**建议风格：** 清脆、有力的建造声

```
Short, satisfying wooden construction sound effect, hammer hitting wood beams, one or two quick impacts, crisp and punchy, indicates a tower has been built
```

---

### 2. 升级防御塔
**使用场景：** 玩家点击已有的塔，选择升级
**触发位置：** `tower_base.gd` 第75行
**建议风格：** 明亮、上扬的魔法/强化音效

```
Magical upgrade sound effect, bright and sparkly ascending chime with a sense of empowerment, indicates a tower has been strengthened and improved
```

---

### 3. 出售防御塔
**使用场景：** 玩家点击已有的塔，选择出售
**触发位置：** `tower_base.gd` 第84行
**建议风格：** 金币入账的清脆声

```
Coin clinking sound effect, cash register cha-ching, satisfying money sound, indicates gold has been earned from selling a tower
```

---

### 4. 箭塔攻击
**使用场景：** 箭塔锁定目标并发射箭矢
**触发位置：** `tower_base.gd` 第157行
**建议风格：** 嗖嗖的射箭声，短促有力

```
Arrow flying through air sound effect, quick whoosh and release, sharp and fast, indicates an arrow has been shot from a tower
```

---

### 5. 加农炮攻击
**使用场景：** 加农炮锁定目标并开火
**触发位置：** `tower_base.gd` 第157行
**建议风格：** 轰隆的炮声，厚重有冲击力

```
Cannon fire explosion sound effect, deep boom and impact, powerful and bassy, indicates a cannon has been fired from a tower
```

---

### 6. 冰塔攻击
**使用场景：** 冰塔锁定目标并发射冰冻魔法
**触发位置：** `tower_base.gd` 第157行
**建议风格：** 冰晶碎裂或魔法冻结的音效

```
Ice crystal magic sound effect, freezing spell effect, crisp and magical, indicates an ice tower has attacked and slowed enemies
```

---

### 7. 怪物被击杀
**使用场景：** 怪物血量归0，死亡时
**触发位置：** `monster_base.gd` 第151行
**建议风格：** 短促的死亡音效

```
Monster death sound effect, short grunt or small explosion, satisfying kill confirmation, indicates an enemy has been defeated
```

---

### 8. 新一波怪物开始
**使用场景：** 新一波怪物即将/开始生成时
**触发位置：** 游戏内波次管理器
**建议风格：** 警报/提示音

```
Alert sound effect, medieval horn or bell, indicates a new wave of monsters is starting, attention-grabbing but not jarring
```

---

### 9. 关卡胜利（短音效版）
**使用场景：** 胜利时的额外提示音（配合BGM）
**建议风格：** 简短的胜利音

```
Short victory sting, celebratory chime or fanfare snippet, quick rewarding sound for winning the level
```

---

### 10. 关卡失败（短音效版）
**使用场景：** 失败时的额外提示音（配合BGM）
**建议风格：** 简短的失败音

```
Short defeat sting, somber but brief chime, quick sound indicating the level has been lost
```

---

## 💡 使用建议总结

| 音频类型 | 推荐工具 |
|---------|----------|
| **BGM（4首）** | **Suno** 或 **Udio** - 完美胜任 |
| **SFX（10个）** | **AudioGen** / **Stable Audio** 或 **Freesound.org** - Suno不太适合做纯音效 |
